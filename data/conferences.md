## How to use this file

**Monitoring windows** — Wren starts watching for competitor activity this many weeks before each event:
- Tier A conferences: 8 weeks before
- Tier B conferences: 4 weeks before

**What Wren looks for:**
- Sponsorship announcements (firm website, conference website, press releases)
- Speaker confirmations (session listings, conference programs)
- Pre-conference content published targeting attendees (blog posts, guides, LinkedIn)
- LinkedIn Ad Library — paid promotions targeting conference audience
- Post-conference recaps and follow-up content

**Signal rule:** Any gated asset published around a conference → flag for behavioral-pattern-analysis.md
